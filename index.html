About Me

Growing up with sybarite parents, I traveled the world, from Bordeaux to Venice, from Quebec to New Orleans, tasting the best food of the European, North & South American cultures.

After finishing my college degree, I emigrated to Spain, where I discovered my PASSION: being a wine love spreader. Twelve years have passed since I started this journey.

With two wine stores and more than 400 hosted tastings behind my back, I’ve gained a wealth of wine expertise and I’m now ready to share this knowledge through humor and casual interactions across the internet ocean!

Welcome to Amo Vino! I’m inviting you to join me in my love and passion for vino. Inspired in the way all my English-speaking people say “I love wine” in Spanish!

“Yo Amo Vino” is a new adventure, and you are more than welcome to surf this wave with me! Don’t forget your glass of wine and your joyful spirit!
